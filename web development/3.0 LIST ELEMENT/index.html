CINNAMON ROLL RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

For the dough :

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 teaspoon yeast
  • granilated sugar
  • egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling :

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablesppon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast ,sugar,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combned into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
